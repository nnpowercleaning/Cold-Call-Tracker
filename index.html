Call script
Opener

"Hi, is this [Name]? Great — my name is [Your Name] with N&N Powercleaning. I'm a local power washing company serving [their area]. I'm not trying to take a lot of your time — I'm just reaching out to see if you've been thinking about getting your driveway, siding, or patio cleaned up this season."

Pause — let them respond

"We're booking jobs in [area] right now. Do you have maybe 60 seconds so I can tell you what we offer?"

Value pitch

"We use professional-grade equipment — not hardware store stuff. We soft wash siding so there's zero risk of damage, and we handle driveways, decks, gutters, the whole exterior. Most jobs take 1–3 hours and the difference is night and day."

Social proof

"We've been doing work right in [their neighborhood] — neighbors have been really happy. We always take before & after photos so you see exactly what you're getting."

Price anchor

"A typical driveway runs $100–$150. Full house wash is $175–$275 depending on size. We do bundle deals if you want a few things done at once."

Trial close

"Does that sound like something you'd want done this season?"

If yes

"Great! I just need your address and I'll send a free quote by end of day. What's the best email or text number for you?"

If hesitant

"No pressure at all — can I text you our info so you have it handy? We book up fast once the season hits, so good to get in early."

Voicemail script (keep under 25 sec)

"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from N&N Powercleaning — local power washing out of Schererville. Reaching out to homeowners in [area] about exterior cleaning this season. We do driveways, siding, decks — all of it. Give me a call at [number], or I'll try you in a couple days. Thanks!"

Rules

Say your number slowly. Never leave more than 2 voicemails per contact. Always follow up with a text after.

Objection handlers
"I'm not interested."
"Totally fair — is it bad timing, or something about the service? I just want to make sure I'm not wasting your time in the future."
"How much does it cost?"
"Depends on what you need — driveway is around $100–$150, full house wash $175–$275. I can give you an exact free quote if you share your address."
"I already have someone."
"That's great — if they're doing a good job, stick with them! But if they're ever unavailable, keep our number handy."
"I'll think about it."
"Of course. Can I send you a quick text with our info? And is there a better time to follow up — maybe a week from now?"
"Are you insured?"
"Great question — we carry full general liability insurance. I can send proof of insurance with your quote if you'd like."
"Can you just email me something?"
"Sure! What's your email? And so I send the right info — is it mainly the driveway, siding, or the full exterior you were thinking about?"
Pro tips
Best call times: Tue–Thu, 9–11am and 4–6pm. Avoid Mondays before 10am and Fridays after 3pm.
The 3-attempt rule: Try a lead 3 times (call, VM, text) before marking cold. Most people answer on attempt 2 or 3.
Speed to lead: Call back web inquiries within 5 minutes. Response speed wins more jobs than price.
